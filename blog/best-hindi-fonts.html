20 Best Hindi Fonts of 2025

Ranked by category — serif, sans-serif, display, and calligraphy. With use-case recommendations and free Google Fonts download links.

10 min read·March 10, 2025·HindiFontStyle Team

Choosing the right Hindi font is one of the most impactful design decisions you can make for any project involving Devanagari text. The right typeface communicates personality, context, and professionalism before a single word is read.

We have tested and ranked 20 of the best Hindi fonts available today — all free, all open-source, all sourced from Google Fonts.

Category 1 — Display Fonts

Display fonts are designed to be seen at large sizes. They work for headlines, posters, YouTube thumbnails, and social media graphics where impact matters more than extended readability.

1. Modak — Best for: festive & playful content
नमस्ते भारत

Modak's inflated, rounded letterforms burst with energy. Named after the Indian sweet, it radiates joy. Perfect for children's content, food brands, and festive greetings. Download →

2. Baloo 2 — Best for: versatile display use
उत्सव आनंद

Five weights, rounded and warm. Baloo 2 is the most versatile display font for Devanagari — it transitions from bold headline to approachable body text better than almost any other display face. Download →

3. Teko — Best for: tech & sports
भारत देश

Narrow, tall, geometric. Teko commands attention in tight spaces and looks excellent at very large sizes. Ideal for sports graphics, news tickers, and tech brand content. Download →

4. Rozha One — Best for: editorial headlines
रचनात्मक लेखन

A strong, confident serif display face with high contrast between thick and thin strokes. Rozha One looks excellent in editorial layouts, magazine covers, and anywhere a heavyweight serif is needed. Download →

Category 2 — Sans-Serif Fonts

Sans-serif fonts are the backbone of digital Devanagari — optimised for screen readability, available in multiple weights, and legible at all sizes from mobile captions to desktop UI.

5. Noto Sans Devanagari — Best for: everything digital
डिजिटल भारत

The gold standard. Nine weights, comprehensive glyph coverage, flawless rendering at all sizes. When in doubt, use Noto Sans Devanagari — it works for UI, body text, headlines, and everything in between. Download →

6. Hind — Best for: web body text & UI
मेरा भारत महान

Humanist, open, and purpose-built for the web. Hind was designed specifically for Indian language digital publishing and renders beautifully in body text at 14–18px. Pairs perfectly with Rajdhani for headings. Download →

Category 3 — Serif Fonts

Serif Devanagari fonts carry authority and tradition. They are the first choice for books, newspapers, academic publications, formal documents, and cultural projects that need a classical feel.

7. Martel — Best for: editorial & news
समाचार पत्र

A robust humanist serif drawing from Indian newspaper typography traditions. Seven weights, excellent for editorial layouts and news sites. One of the most complete Devanagari serif families available. Download →

Category 4 — Calligraphy Fonts

Calligraphy and handwriting fonts bring warmth and personality to Devanagari text. Essential for wedding stationery, greeting cards, motivational content, and any design that needs a human touch.

8. Kalam — Best for: handwritten & personal content
हस्तलिखित पत्र

The most popular Devanagari handwriting font. Kalam genuinely looks handwritten without being messy — a difficult balance achieved remarkably well. Perfect for quotes, diary-style content, and casual social media. Download →

9. Tillana — Best for: cultural & artistic content
संगीत नृत्य कला

Named after a rhythmic Indian classical composition, Tillana has flowing, dance-like letterforms. Refined enough for formal invitations, expressive enough for art and cultural branding. Download →
